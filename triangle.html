While there are only rough and vague categories of pets.
I'll still try to give a basic understanding of why we call pets a certain way.

Tanks are pets based on tons of HPHealth and PRMR Resistances, doing as their name would suggest- tanking.

  • lobbot giraffbot Pure Tanks are the most extreme of tanks, allocating all their stats into HP,PR, and MR.
    In current meta, they're commonly seen holding critically important weapons while being replenished with WP.
  • slothbot dinobot WP Tanks are similar to pure tanks, with some added WPWP
    On teams where you can't count on replenishment, choosing a pet with extra WP is the only way you can ensure reliable procs of your weapons.
  • gorilla snail Hybrid Tanks, true to their name, are Hybrids of Attackers & Tanks, usually performing multipurpose roles.
    A Gorilla with Shield for example, keeps taunt taunting the enemy, and thus protects its team, but also attacks with its strength on off-turns.

Attackers and Supporters are pets based mostly on STRStrength or MAGMagic, as these are the stats that their weapons act upon.

  • # Attackers
  • A pet holding a sword for example, deals damage based on it's strength, so it makes sense to maximise this stat as much as possible. The same also applies to the magical side of pets too.
  • And there's even attackers that have both high strength and magic, such as monkeyMonkey! But these pets are usually considered quite useless, as the only weapons that can make use of such stats are runeRune and lruneLrune, which also work with normal strength or magical attackers.
  • # Supporters
  • An attacker can always count on massive replenishment from mtap, and even if that doesn't work, an attacker not acting is likely not going to end in a lost battle.
    But if your healer suddenly stops acting, that could very quickly end up disastrous. So in similar logic to WP tanks, supporters have some added WP to ensure their acting, giving up some power in exchange.
    • MAGMagic-based supporters are commonly called Healers and Replenishers,
    • STRStrength-based supporters commonly hold buff and debuff weapons like Banner & Axe.

Other useful terms:

  • Gems are pets with near zero, or zero stats in tankiness, and are usually considered the glass cannons of the game.
  • Bots are pets with zeroed strength and magic, which makes them uniquely suited to tanking.
