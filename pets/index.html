Fox has base stats of:

  • 4 Health
  • 9 Strength
  • 1 Physical Resistance
  • 3 Weapon Points (basically Mana)
  • 1 Magic
  • 2 Magical resistance

What I see in these stats, is a tanky strength-based attacker, with a little bit of WP.
⇒ I also see 20 stats overall, Fox is a 20-Stat.
Pet stats are often talked about in this format: Hp.Str.Pr.Wp.Mag.Mr. Thus, our Fox would be a 4.9.1.3.1.2. For simpler pets, like 8.1.1.8.1.1, people also often leave out the unimportant ones, calling the pet an "8 8".

What do all these numbers mean though? My Fox has different numbers!
Well, these base stats don't tell the whole story, they're just used to calculate your actual stats.
The formulas to calculate your actual stats look like this:
(Passives can affect these stats too, but I'll talk about them in passives.) 

 500 + 2 * Lvl * Stat = HP      

 100 +     Lvl * Stat = STR     

 25  + 2 * Lvl * Stat = iPR     


 500 + 2 * Lvl * Stat = WP      

 100 +     Lvl * Stat = MAG     

 25  + 2 * Lvl * Stat = iMR

Hence, a level 20 fox would have:

 500 + 2 * 20  * 4    = 660 HP  

 100 +     20  * 9    = 280 STR 

 25  + 2 * 20  * 1    = 65  iPR 


 500 + 2 * 20  * 3    = 620 WP  

 100 +     20  * 1    = 120 MAG 

 25  + 2 * 20  * 2    = 105 iMR

These are now all your stats! ...well with a little asterisk, the resistance values you're seeing are your internal resistances. To convert them to usable percentages, we do this calculation:

 0.8 (iPR) / (100 + iPR) = PR     

 0.8 (iMR) / (100 + iMR) = MR     


 0.8 ( 65) / (100 +  65) = 0.3152
⇒ 31.5% PR 
 0.8 (105) / (100 + 105) = 0.4098
⇒ 41% MR
